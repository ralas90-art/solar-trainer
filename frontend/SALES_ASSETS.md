# [Brand] Sales Demo Playbook

This document provides a structured narrative for delivering a high-impact, 5-minute demo of the [Brand] Sales Training OS.

## 1. The Opening (30 Seconds)
**Goal:** Establish the problem of "Invisible Sales Gaps".

> "Most sales managers have a blind spot. They know their team isn't hitting quota, but they don't know *why*. They see the low numbers, but they don't see the specific missed objections or the lack of practice. [Brand] changes that by making sales performance visible and coachable in real-time."

## 2. The Command Center (90 Seconds)
**Path:** `/manager`

**What to highlight:**
- **Team Roster:** Show the different statuses (Top Performer, Needs Attention, At Risk).
- **Coaching Signals:** Point to a specific signal (e.g., Alex Drift's "Practice Avoidance").
- **Narrative:** "Look at Alex. He's a great rep, but our AI detected he's avoiding the 'Objection Handling' practice module. Instead of waiting for a bad month, you can intervene *now*."

## 3. The Rep Experience (90 Seconds)
**Path:** `/dashboard/reps/sarah-sun` (or similar)

**What to highlight:**
- **Skill Mastery Radar:** Show the visual breakdown of skills.
- **Simulation History:** Show the recent scores and the 'Master' level.
- **Narrative:** "This is Sarah. She's our top performer. You can see exactly which frameworks she's mastered. We can now take her 'winning patterns' and clone them across the rest of your team."

## 4. The AI Simulator (60 Seconds)
**Path:** `/ai-simulator`

**What to highlight:**
- **Safe Environment:** "Reps practice here, not on your leads."
- **Instant Feedback:** "The AI provides immediate grading on sentiment, framework adherence, and objection handling."

## 5. The Close (30 Seconds)
**Goal:** Define the ROI.

> "[Brand] isn't just a training tool; it's a management system. It reduces ramp time for new hires by 40% and ensures your veterans never get complacent. Shall we set up a trial for your regional team?"

---

## Objection Handling Playbook

### "We already have a CRM/LMS."
**Response:** "CRMs track results; [Brand] tracks *readiness*. Most LMS systems are passive videos that reps ignore. [Brand] is interactive practice that reps *can't* ignore because their performance is visible to you."

### "My reps are too busy to practice."
**Response:** "That's exactly why they need this. If they're too busy to practice, they're likely making expensive mistakes on live leads. 10 minutes of AI simulation is worth 2 hours of 'learning by doing' on your dime."

### "Is this just for new hires?"
**Response:** "Not at all. Look at Sarah in our demo—she's a 'Master' level rep. She uses it to sharpen her edge on new objections or complex frameworks. It's about maintaining elite performance, not just basic training."

---

## Technical Safety Note
**Important:** During the demo, you can click anything, but the system is in **Read-Only Demo Mode**. No actual data will be saved to the database, ensuring your demo environment remains clean and consistent for every prospect.
