**Trainer Script:**
"Welcome to Day 1 of the Solar Sales Accelerator. By Day 7, you won’t just be a trainee, you will be field-ready, capable of running a full appointment cycle independently. But first, we need to cast a vision. 
[PAUSE] *Ask: Why are you here? What is your personal mission?*

"This industry isn't just about money, but the money is real if you put in the work. Let me break down a realistic path to $100K+ in your first year:
- **Month 1:** $3,000 to $5,000. You are learning the curve. This is 'the dip'. 
- **Month 3:** $8,000 to $12,000. Competence sets in. The math starts working for you.
- **Month 6+:** $15,000 to $25,000+. This is mastery. 

"We want you at mastery. But to get there, you must survive the 4 Stages of Competence. Right now, you are in 'Unconscious Incompetence'. Soon, you will hit 'Conscious Incompetence'—the dip where it gets hard. Push through that, and you reach mastery."
**Activity:** Play the Top Performer Spotlight (a 3-min video testimonial from a veteran rep sharing their journey and "Why").
**Transition Statement:** "Now that you know *why* we're here and *where* we are going, let's get you administratively locked in so there are zero distractions moving forward."

#### Module 1.2 – Administrative Onboarding (45-60 min)
**Trainer Script:**
"The quickest way to fail in sales is to have administrative chaos. For the next hour, we are getting every system set up. When you leave this room, you are a fully functioning consultant."
*Guide the room through the checklist step-by-step. Do not move to the next item until every trainee gives a 'Thumbs Up'.*
**Trainee Questions & Answers:**
*Q: "When do my business cards arrive?"* A: "They are ordered today and will be on your desk by Day 5."
*Q: "Do I need a pro license for Aurora?"* A: "We handle your enterprise seat; just use the login sent to your email."
**Transition Statement:** "You are officially in the system. Now let's talk about the culture of the team you just joined."

#### Module 1.3 – Company Culture & Expectations (20 min)
**Trainer Script:**
"Culture is defined by what we tolerate. Here is the daily standard:
- 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Admin time, following up, routing your day.
- 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Team calls and training.
- 2:00 PM - 7:00 PM: The Golden Hours. Non-negotiable prospecting time. You are on the doors or on the phones. 

"Our Weekly KPIs are the heartbeat of your business:
100 to 150 doors knocked or cold calls made. Setting 10 to 15 appointments. Sitting 5 to 8 of those. Closing 1 to 3 deals by week 3. It’s strictly a numbers game until your skill increases.
[PAUSE] *Ask: How does this volume compare to expectations you had coming in?*

"You will be supported. You get daily check-ins for your first two weeks, ride-alongs with veterans, and a dedicated Slack channel."
*(Review the compensation structure briefly, emphasizing the chargeback policy for cancellations).*
**Transition Statement:** "This wraps our morning. Take lunch, and come back at ready to dive into the technical meat of the solar industry."

### Afternoon Session (3-4 hours)

#### Module 1.4 – Solar Industry Overview (20 min)
**Trainer Script:**
"Welcome back. Why solar? And why *now*? U.S. residential solar is exploding because utility bills are inflating at an average of 6-8% annually. 
"Homeowners go solar for three reasons: Utility inflation protection, energy independence, and increasing their home value. 
"You are competing with Tesla, Sunrun, and local installers. Your edge is the customized consultation. 
"Remember the 30% ITC. It extends through 2032. [LEGAL REVIEW] It is a tax liability offset, not a stimulus check.
"Let's debunk some myths: 'Solar doesn't work locally because of clouds.' False, Germany leads the world in solar and has the climate of Alaska. 'It's too expensive.' False, it is zero down—a bill swap."
**Transition Statement:** "Now that we know *why* it works economically, let's look at *how* it works technologically."

#### Module 1.5 – Solar Technology Fundamentals (25 min)
**Trainer Script:**
"Here is the simple flow you will explain to homeowners. Photons hit the panel, generating Direct Current (DC) electricity. The Inverter converts that DC to Alternating Current (AC), which is what powers the home. Excess power goes to the grid, banking net metering credits. At night, the grid supplies power back. 
[PAUSE] *Ask: Can a homeowner run their house during a blackout if they don't have a battery?*
*(Answer: No, the inverter shuts down to prevent sending live power to the grid while linemen are working).*

"System components: Monocrystalline panels (black, efficient) vs polycrystalline (blue, older). We use String or Microinverters. Racking, Monitoring, and the Meter. 
"Panel efficiency is usually 16-22%. They degrade at 0.5% to 1% a year—this is factored into the savings. 
"What solar does NOT do: It won't eliminate the connection fee. It won't power their home in an outage without a battery. It won't pay them cash monthly unless we are in an SREC state."
**Transition Statement:** "You now understand the product. Let's fix how you sell it."

#### Module 1.6 – Identity Shift: Consultant vs Salesperson (20 min)
**Trainer Script:**
"Repeat after me: I am not selling panels. I am restructuring utility spending and protecting families from inflation.
[PAUSE] *Have the room repeat the phrase.*

"A salesperson chases commission and pushes products. A consultant diagnoses problems and prescribes solutions. You will face rejection. Top reps hear 'no' 100 times a week. 'No' means 'not right now' or 'I don't understand yet'. Do not take it personally. Celebrate the small wins—getting an appointment, even if it doesn't close today, means your system is working. 
"Let's look at the Integrity Creed in your workbook. We never promise guaranteed savings we can't back up. We never exaggerate the ROI. We disqualify aggressively. A bad fit today is a cancellation tomorrow."
**Transition Statement:** "A true consultant diagnoses with data. Our data is the utility bill. Let's learn to read it."

#### Module 1.7 – Utility Bill Mastery (Introduction) (25 min)
**Trainer Script:**
"The utility bill is the homeowner's pain point. It is our diagnostic tool.
*Project a sample bill on the screen.*
"Look for the account number and service address. Look at the total kWh usage. Look at the total charges. Is the rate tiered or Time-Of-Use (TOU)? 
"Here is your qualification criteria snippet: You want 600+ kWh monthly. A bill over $100. Seasonal consistency. 
"Red flags: Past-due balances indicate credit risk. Budget billing hides their true usage. Low usage in a mansion usually means they are never home or moving soon. 
"Offset percentage: 100% offset means the system produces exactly what they use annually. 120% prepares them for an EV. 80% means they will still have a small utility bill. Understand the True-up logic for NEM."
**Transition Statement:** "We have covered massive ground today. Let's summarize and set the board for tomorrow."

#### Module 1.8 – Day 1 Wrap-Up & Preview (15 min)
**Trainer Script:**
"You stayed locked in today. Great work. 
You are administratively onboarded. You understand the tech, you can read a bill, and you know your identity is a Consultant. 
Tonight's homework is mandatory. You will watch two videos on Net Metering, read the top 5 objection handling guide, and record yourself explaining solar to a family member. We will check this tomorrow morning. 
Tomorrow, we hit Prospecting, Door Knocking Mastery, and live role-play. See you at 10 AM sharp."

## 4. ROLE-PLAY SCENARIOS

### Bill Analysis Drill (Module 1.7)
**Setup:** Trainees break into groups of three. Give each group 5 sample utility bills of varying quality (Good fit, Marginal, Disqualified).
**The Task:** They have 10 minutes to analyze the bills and present their findings to the trainer.
**Success Criteria:**
- Successfully identifies the "Red Flag" (e.g., past due balance) on Bill #3.
- correctly reads total 12-month trailing kWh on Bill #1.
- Accurately states that Bill #4 (Budget Billing) needs the true usage pulled from the utility portal.

## 5. ASSESSMENT MATERIALS

### Solar Industry Literacy & Tech Quiz (Combo Module 1.4 & 1.5)
*(Target: 80% passing grade required)*

1. What is the current federal ITC percentage and when does it step down/sunset (under current IRA)?
2. What is the primary difference between a string inverter and a microinverter?
3. If a system is rated at 100% offset, what does that mean practically for the homeowner over exactly 12 months?
4. A homeowner says, "I don't need solar, my power rarely goes out." What is the Consultant response?
5. True or False: Solar panels degrade at approximately 0.5% to 1% per year.

**Answer Key Overview:**
1. 30%, through 2032. (Must note it offsets liability).
2. String inverters act like old Christmas lights (single point of failure); microinverters optimize each panel independently.
3. It produces exactly the amount of kWh the home consumed in the previous 12 months.
4. "Solar isn't just a generator for blackouts—without a battery, it actually shuts off during a blackout anyway. Solar is about stopping the 8% annual inflation of your utility bill."
5. True.
