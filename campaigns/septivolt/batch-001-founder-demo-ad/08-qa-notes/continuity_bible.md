# Campaign Visual Continuity Bible: Batch 001

This visual continuity guide ensures all generated images, video frames, and UI assets adhere to the active **SeptiVolt Brand Guidelines**.

---

## 1. Core Brand Colors (Solar Dawn Theme)
We use the active brand design tokens mapped from the website (`globals.css` base theme):
*   **Primary Accent (Solar Orange):** `#F97316` (used for primary highlights, outline glow, active HUD corners, and prominent buttons).
*   **Secondary Accent (Amber/Gold):** `#F59E0B` (used for secondary accents, warning badges, and scoring meters).
*   **Base Background (Dark Slate / Charcoal):** `#121212` and `#1A1A1A` (used for deep dark slate backgrounds and solid charcoal panel containers).
*   **Text Cues (Conductive Silver / Gray):** `#94A3B8` and `#F8FAFC`.

---

## 2. Character Guidelines
Only use fictional, AI-generated characters to avoid licensing issues:
*   **Young Sales Representative:** Fictional 25-30 years old, clean-cut, wearing casual professional attire (e.g., dark navy or dark charcoal polo shirt with a small orange/amber badge on the left chest), focused or engaged expression.
*   **Experienced Sales Manager:** Fictional 40-45 years old, professional attire, standing or sitting in a modern office, confident leadership stance.
*   **Founder-style Presenter:** Approachable, confident, direct eye contact with the camera, dressed in a sleek dark grey t-shirt or modern blazer.
*   **Simulated Homeowner (UI):** Fictional homeowners (e.g., middle-aged couple, skeptical but open expressions) rendered inside the UI preview screens.

---

## 3. Environmental Rules
All backgrounds must feel realistic and professional:
*   **Modern Sales Office:** Structured open-plan workspaces, clean glass desks, neutral-colored walls with warm lighting glows, background screens with generic chart silhouettes.
*   **Training Room:** Sleek seminar room with whiteboards containing draft solar diagrams (kW/kWh conversions, grid interfaces), sales reps holding modern tablets.
*   **Solar Context Cues:** Desktop proposals, generic utility bills on desks, monocrystalline black solar panels on roofs visible in the background, or solar design layouts on tablets.
*   *Avoid:* Messy/unsafe environments, recognizable private houses, visible real street numbers, or unsafe installer work practices.

---

## 4. UI & Dashboard Guidelines
When rendering the training interface on screens:
*   **Layout Style:** Dark mode interface with charcoal panels, glowing Solar Orange borders, and Amber progress indicators.
*   **Key Interface Sections:**
    *   *AI Roleplay (Objections Drill):* Dialogue cards showing simulated text exchange between rep and homeowner.
    *   *Coaching Report:* Numeric score HUD (e.g., "88/100") with detailed rating scales.
    *   *Manager Hub:* Team analytics showing progress tracks and caution alert icons for struggling reps.
    *   *Certification Track:* Progress bars rendering in glowing Amber/Orange.
*   **Data Integrity:** All text inside mockups must be generic, fictional, or unreadable to prevent false-data flags.
