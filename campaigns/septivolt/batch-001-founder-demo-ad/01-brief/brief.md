# Campaign Brief: SeptiVolt Founder Demo Ad (Batch 001)

## 1. Project & Campaign Objectives
*   **Project:** SeptiVolt (AI Solar Sales Training OS)
*   **Campaign Goal:** Create a structured set of Google-safe, high-converting visual and text assets to promote SeptiVolt's interactive simulator, automated coaching reports, and manager visibility tools.
*   **Primary Distribution Channels:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn, Cold Email/Loom, and Landing Page Hero.

## 2. Brand Positioning Guidelines
*   **Core Message:** Move from outdated PDF training scripts and shadowing to interactive, voice-conversational AI training simulations.
*   **Maturity Stance:** Position SeptiVolt as functional, pilot-ready, and actively developing.
*   *Do Not Overclaim:* Do not guarantee sales numbers or pitch the platform as a mature enterprise legacy suite.
*   *Approved Phrases:*
    *   "AI training OS for solar sales teams"
    *   "designed to support faster ramp-up"
    *   "built to help reps practice"
    *   "helps managers see training progress"
    *   "pilot-ready"
    *   "early-access platform"

## 3. Target Audience
*   **Primary:** Solar company owners, EPC sales leaders, sales managers, recruiting directors, D2D team leaders, and virtual sales teams.
*   **Secondary:** Solar industry influencers, independent sales trainers, and bilingual team leaders.

## 4. Visual Style & Brand Colors
Consistent with `septivolt.com` active design tokens:
*   **Visual Direction:** "Solar Dawn" HUD style. Dark Slate backgrounds, glowing Solar Orange outlines, and Amber progress elements.
*   **Colors to Use:** Solar Orange (`#F97316`), Amber (`#F59E0B`), and Dark Slate/Charcoal (`#121212`, `#1A1A1A`).

## 5. Google AI Safety & Claim Compliance
*   **Compliance Status:** SAFE
*   **Safety Constraints:**
    *   Fictional reps, fictional dashboard graphics, and realistic scenarios.
    *   No celebrity likenesses, third-party logos, or protected IP.
    *   No false income claims or deceptive performance guarantees.
*   *Claim Shielding:*
    *   Avoid: "guaranteed to double close rates," "reps will make $10k more," or "the #1 platform."
    *   Prefer: "designed to help reps practice," "built to improve training consistency," and "helps managers see rep progress."
