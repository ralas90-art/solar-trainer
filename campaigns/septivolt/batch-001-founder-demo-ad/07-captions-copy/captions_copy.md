## 5. Platform Repurpose Guide

### TikTok / Instagram Reels (9:16 Vertical)
*   **Best Hook:** *"Most solar reps are practicing on real prospects."*
*   **Duration:** 8–20 seconds.
*   **Visual Strategy:** Fast visual hook, product screen demo, and simple CTA.

### Facebook Reels / Meta Feed Ad (9:16 / 4:5 Square)
*   **Best Hook:** *"Your reps should not learn objection handling after losing the deal."*
*   **Duration:** 15–30 seconds.
*   **Visual Strategy:** Pain (frustrated rep) → Solution (AI roleplay simulation) → Demo CTA.

### LinkedIn (16:9 Desktop)
*   **Best Hook:** *"Solar training is still too dependent on scripts, shadowing, and inconsistent manager feedback."*
*   **Duration:** 30–45 seconds.
*   **Visual Strategy:** Founder-led strategic pitch with dashboard overlays.

### YouTube Shorts (9:16 Vertical)
*   **Best Hook:** *"What if every solar rep could practice before the next sales call?"*
*   **Duration:** 20–30 seconds.
*   **Visual Strategy:** Clean founder demo clip with screen share of simulation feedback.
