# Campaign Asset Manifest: SeptiVolt Founder Demo Ad (Batch 001)

This manifest serves as the index and organization guide for the **SeptiVolt Founder Demo Ad (Batch 001)** campaign. It maps all strategy briefs, copywriting assets, visual prompts, and continuity guidelines to their respective directory locations.

---

## 1. Campaign Brief Summary
*   **Brand:** SeptiVolt
*   **Campaign Goal:** Promote SeptiVolt as an AI Solar Sales Training Operating System using Google-safe, high-converting visual and text assets.
*   **Target Audience:** Solar company owners, EPC sales managers, recruiting directors, D2D team leads, and bilingual sales consultants.
*   **Key Message:** Transition teams from outdated static training methods (PDF scripts, shadowing) to voice-conversational AI training simulations that help reps practice before real client calls.
*   **Brand Styling:** "Solar Dawn" HUD styling. Dark Slate backgrounds, glowing Solar Orange outlines, and Amber progress elements.
*   **Compliance Gate:** 100% compliant with Google AI Safety and Claim guidelines (practice-oriented phrasing, fictional UI screens, no celebrity likenesses, no deceptive income promises).

*   **Hook 2:** *"Your reps should not learn objection handling after losing the deal."* (For Facebook Reels / Feed ads)
*   **Caption Tone A (Direct/Professional):** Best for LinkedIn and Facebook. Highlights scripting issues, shadowing, and structured AI simulation ramp-up.
