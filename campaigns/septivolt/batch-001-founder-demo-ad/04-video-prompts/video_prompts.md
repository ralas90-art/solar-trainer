# Gemini/Veo Video Prompts: Batch 001

This log details the 3 video prompts designed for Gemini/Veo image-to-video animation. Each prompt produces a 6–8 second vertical (9:16) video anchored on the Flow base images with correct `septivolt.com` branding colors.

---

## Video Prompt 1: Rep Practice Moment
*   **Source Image Anchor:** Image 1 (The Undertrained Solar Rep)
*   **Compliance Status:** SAFE (fictional rep, simulator progress display)
*   **Google-Safe Video Prompt:**
    > "Animate the selected image of the young solar sales rep at the desk. Create a 6-second vertical 9:16 video. Begin with a slow push-in toward the rep looking at the laptop. The laptop screen softly animates with a fictional AI roleplay interface: objection handling score, training progress, and certification progress. The rep shifts from slightly overwhelmed to focused and confident. Add subtle movement in the office background, gentle screen glow, and realistic lighting. Keep the motion smooth, cinematic, and professional. Use a premium dark slate and charcoal styling with glowing Solar Orange UI outlines (#F97316) and Amber progress indicators (#F59E0B). No readable private data. No real brand logos. No exaggerated holograms. End with the rep clicking “Start Practice” on the fictional dashboard. --aspect 9:16"
*   **On-Screen Text Overlay:** *"Train before the real sales call."*
*   **Avoid List:**
    > `unrealistic sci-fi effects, fake income claims, real customer data, celebrity likenesses, guaranteed performance language`
