# **THE \[YOUR COMPANY\]**

# **INTEGRITY CREED**

---

This document constitutes a formal professional standards agreement between the undersigned Sales Representative and \[YOUR COMPANY\]. By signing below, the Representative affirms their unconditional commitment to the principles, conduct standards, and operational expectations outlined herein.

---

#### **PREAMBLE**

\[YOUR COMPANY\] is built on a culture of integrity, professionalism, and service. We do not compete on deception. We compete on expertise, trust, and the genuine delivery of value to homeowners. Every action taken in the field is a direct reflection of this organization's reputation. The standards below are not optional guidelines — they are the minimum baseline for continued representation of \[YOUR COMPANY\] and its mission.

---

#### **REPRESENTATIVE COMMITMENTS**

*By placing a check in each box below and signing this document, I individually affirm each of the following commitments without reservation:*

---

**I. Commitment to the Golden Hours**

* **I commit to the Golden Hours.**

I understand that the hours between 2:00 PM and 7:00 PM represent the highest-probability window for homeowner contact and appointment conversion. I commit to being actively deployed in the field during these hours, and I will not allow non-revenue-generating activities to displace this time without prior authorization from my Team Lead or Manager.

---

**II. Commitment to Truthful Representations**

* **I will not promise free solar or guaranteed tax cash-returns.**

I understand that solar energy systems are not free, and that federal and state tax incentives are subject to individual eligibility and professional tax assessment. I will never represent the Investment Tax Credit (ITC) or any other incentive as a guaranteed cash return, a rebate, or a payment. I will always direct homeowners to consult a qualified tax professional regarding their personal tax situation.

---

**III. Commitment to Data-Driven Qualification**

* **I will qualify aggressively using utility data.**

I commit to requesting and reviewing actual utility bill data from every prospective customer before proceeding to proposal. I will use real kWh consumption figures, current rate schedules, and 12-month billing history to build accurate system designs and savings projections. I will not present proposals based on estimated or assumed usage.

---

#### **ACKNOWLEDGEMENT & AGREEMENT**

I, the undersigned, have read and fully understood the commitments set forth in this Integrity Creed. I enter into this agreement voluntarily, and I understand that failure to uphold these standards may result in disciplinary action, termination of my Independent Contractor Agreement, or removal from \[YOUR COMPANY\]'s sales program at the sole discretion of management.
