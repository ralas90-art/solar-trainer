# Solar Sales Training Curriculum Breakdown

This document provides a detailed breakdown of the daily training modules for the Solar Sales Trainer Agent.

---

## Day 1: The Foundation
**Subtitle:** Mindset, Physics & Operations

### 🎯 Mission
**Objective:** Transform from a 'Salesperson' into a 'Consultant'.
By the end of Day 1, you will understand the physics of solar, the psychology of a top performer, and exactly how to qualify a home. This is the foundation of your career.

### 📚 Topics & Content

#### 1. The Learning Curve
*   **Concept:** Understanding the 4 stages of competence to avoid quitting during the "Dip of Despair".
*   **Stages:** Unconscious Incompetence, Conscious Incompetence (The Dip), Conscious Competence (The Grind), Unconscious Competence (Flow State).

#### 2. The History of the Grid
*   **Concept:** The shift from Centralized Power (AC/Edison/Coal) to Decentralized Power (DC/Solar).
*   **Key Takeaway:** The grid is old, inefficient, and expensive. Solar is the inevitable upgrade.

#### 3. The Solar Mindset
*   **Concept:** Reframing Rejection.
*   **Rookie vs Pro:** Rookies take "No" personally. Pros see "No" as a request for more info or a step closer to "Yes".
*   **The Creed:** Integrity First, Service Mindset, Belief.

#### 4. Physics of Savings (PV Basics)
*   **Process:** Generation (DC) -> Conversion (Inverter to AC) -> Consumption (Home use) -> Net Metering (Grid storage).
*   **Tech:** Microinverters (independent panels, safer) vs. String Inverters (single point of failure).

#### 5. Decoding the Bill
*   **Key Concept:** kW (Speed/Horsepower) vs kWh (Volume/Gallons). You pay for kWh.
*   **Tiers:** Avoid Tier 2+ "Penalty Zones".

#### 6. Roof Anatomy 101
*   **Terms:** Rafters (Skeleton), Flashing (Waterproofing), Azimuth (Direction - South is Gold), Pitch (Steepness).
*   **Disqualifiers:** Wood Shake roofs, North-facing only, Zinsco/Federal Pacific panels (Fire hazards).

#### 7. Project Lifecycle
*   **Roadmap:** Sale -> Site Survey -> Engineering/Permitting -> Installation -> Inspection -> PTO (Permission to Operate).

### 🎭 Roleplay Scenarios
*   **(Simulation) The Skeptical Engineer:** Test your technical knowledge on degradation and efficiency.

## Day 2: Prospecting
**Subtitle:** The Hunt & Territory Management

### 🎯 Mission
**Objective:** Master the science of finding qualified homeowners.
Avatar Focus: Territory Management and Efficiency. Stop wasting gas; start maximizing Face Time.

### 📚 Topics & Content

#### 1. Territory Management: The Cloverleaf Strategy
*   **Concept:** Build a web of influence around a sale or install.
*   **Pattern:** Loop 1 (Neighbors) -> Loop 2 (Across St) -> Loop 3 (Next Block).

#### 2. The "Breadcrumb" Strategy
*   **Concept:** Never leave a door un-knocked.
*   **Tactic:** Leave a handwritten-style note (not a flyer) if they aren't home to spark curiosity.

#### 3. The Perfect Day Schedule
*   **Golden Hours:** 3:00 PM - 7:30 PM (When people are home).
*   **Admin Time:** 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM (Scouting, don't knock empty houses).

#### 4. Door-to-Door Mastery (Body Language)
*   **The Trust Zone:** Stand back 6 feet.
*   **Eye Contact:** Break it occasionally to lower pressure.
*   **Reading Signals:** Feet pointing away = they want to leave. Lean away = you are too close.

#### 5. The "Anti-Sales" Approach
*   **Concept:** Lower the "Salesman Alarm".
*   **Script:** Sound like a confused neighbor ("I was just looking for...") rather than a polished salesperson ("Hi! I'm with Solar Bros!").

#### 6. Virtual/Phone Pros (The Air Game)
*   **Tone:** Requires 20% more energy than in-person.
*   **3-Second Rule:** Hook them immediately or get hung up on.
*   **Hooks:** Pattern Interrupt, Neighbor Drop, Mystery, Utility Hook, Wrong Number.

### 🎭 Roleplay Scenarios
*   **(Simulation) Not Interested:** Overcoming the immediate knee-jerk rejection.
*   **(Simulation) I'm Busy:** Pivoting to a scheduled time (The 'Go-Back').

## Day 3: Connection
**Subtitle:** Discovery & Psychology

### 🎯 Mission
**Objective:** Stop being a 'Salesperson' and start being a 'Trusted Advisor'.
Focus on "The Art of Connection" and finding problems rather than pushing products.

### 📚 Topics & Content

#### 1. In-Home Mastery (Positioning)
*   **The Power Seat:** Sit *next* to the homeowner (collaborative), not across from them (adversarial).

#### 2. Personality Types (BOLT System)
*   **Bull (Driver):** Direct, wants results.
*   **Owl (Analytical):** Wants data and details.
*   **Lamb (Amiable):** Wants safety and social proof.
*   **Tiger (Expressive):** Wants status and aesthetics.

#### 3. FBI Negotiation Tactics
*   **Tactical Empathy:** "Late-Night FM DJ Voice" to calm them.
*   **Mirroring:** Repeat the last 3 words as a question.
*   **Labeling:** Validate emotions ("It seems like you're hesitating...").

#### 4. Question Architecture
*   **Open-Ended:** Who, What, Where, When, Why, How. (Gets them talking).
*   **Tie-Downs:** Micro-commitments ("...wouldn't you agree?").
*   **Clarifying:** Digging deeper into vague statements.

#### 5. The "Columbo" Method
*   **Concept:** Act slightly confused/dumb to make the homeowner feel superior and explain things to you.

### 🎭 Roleplay Scenarios
*   **(Simulation) Deep Discovery:** Finding pain points with a skeptical "Owl" (John).
*   **(Simulation) The Price Shopper:** Handling someone who demands price before value.

## Day 4: Presentation
**Subtitle:** The Solution & The Math

### 🎯 Mission
**Objective:** Present the solution so clearly that price becomes irrelevant.
Explain the "Bill Swap" simply and stack the value.

### 📚 Topics & Content

#### 1. Utility Bill Autopsy
*   **Skill:** Dissecting the bill line-by-line to show hidden fees (Transmission, Distribution, Public Purpose Programs).

#### 2. The Value Stack
*   **4 Pillars:** Fixed Cost (Inflation Hedge), Equity (Home Value), Tax Incentives (ITC), Control (Ownership).

#### 3. Inflation Math
*   **Concept:** "Cost of Doing Nothing". Showing how a $200 bill becomes $858 in 25 years vs. a fixed solar payment.

#### 4. Advanced Metering (The Utility Game)
*   **NEM 1.0 vs 3.0:** Understanding Net Metering vs. Net Billing (requiring batteries).
*   **Battery Truths:** Managing expectations on what a battery can actually back up (Critical Load vs Whole Home).

#### 5. Financing Menu
*   **Cash:** Best ROI.
*   **Loan:** Ownership/Asset with $0 down.
*   **PPA:** Pay for Production (No debt, good for retirees).

#### 6. The ROI Script
*   **Pitch:** "Where else can you get a guaranteed, tax-free 10% return?"

### 🎭 Roleplay Scenarios
*   **(Simulation) Bad Experience:** Differentiating from a neighbor's solar nightmare.
*   **(Simulation) Debt Fear:** Overcoming fear of a 25-year loan by reframing as "Redirected Spend".

## Day 5: Closing
**Subtitle:** Objections & Decisions

### 🎯 Mission
**Objective:** Navigate objections and guide the homeowner to a decision.
"Closing" is helping someone make a decision that is good for them.

### 📚 Topics & Content

#### 1. Objection Judo: The Porcupine Technique
*   **Tactic:** Answer a question with a question to uncover the real concern.
*   **Example:** "Does it have a battery?" -> "Are you looking for backup power?"

#### 2. Feel-Felt-Found
*   **Script:** "I understand how you FEEL. Others FELT the same. They FOUND that..."
*   **Applications:** Price, Spousal, Uncertainty, Lien Fear.

#### 3. The Assumptive Close
*   **Tactic:** Focus on *how* to proceed (logistics), not *if*.
*   **Script:** "Which works better for the survey, Tuesday or Wednesday?"

#### 4. The Decision Matrix (Ben Franklin Close)
*   **Visual:** Compare Option A (Do Nothing/Rent/Inflation) vs Option B (Solar/Own/Fixed).

#### 5. Contract Decoder
*   **Skill:** Translating legal terms into human language to reduce anxiety (e.g., "Right to Cancel", "Escalator", "Scope of Work").

#### 6. Ethics
*   **Rules:** Never promise the Tax Credit (it's an offset). Solar is "Zero Down", not "Free".

### 🎭 Roleplay Scenarios
*   **(Simulation) The Price Objection:** Using Feel-Felt-Found.
*   **(Simulation) The Stalling Objection:** "I need to think about it."
*   **(Simulation) The Moving Objection:** "I might move in 5 years."

## Day 6: Mastery
**Subtitle:** Referrals & Automation

### 🎯 Mission
**Objective:** Turn one sale into three through the Referral Engine.
Build a self-sustaining business so you stop cold calling.

### 📚 Topics & Content

#### 1. The Moment of Happiness
*   **Timing:** Ask for referrals when value is highest (Install Day or First Bill), not when asking for money.

#### 2. The Referral Engine / Tree
*   **Concept:** 1 happy customer -> 3 new leads (Neighbors, Friends, Family).

#### 3. Orphan Owner Strategy
*   **Target:** Homeowners with existing solar whose company went bust.
*   **Script:** Lead with service/support. Be the hero.

#### 4. Referral Scripts
*   **Scripts:** "Who Do You Know?", "The Hero Script", "The Bill Kill".

#### 5. Creating Advocates
*   **Tactics:** Social Media tagging, "Solar Warming" gifts, Google Reviews.

### 🎭 Roleplay Scenarios
*   **(Simulation) Asking for Referrals:** Getting introductions without being pushy.
