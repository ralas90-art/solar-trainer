# SeptiVolt: Founder Demo Script (Zoom/Loom)
**Length:** 7–10 minutes
**Tone:** Natural, authoritative, conversational. Speaking directly to a solar owner/sales leader.

---

### [0:00 - 1:00] The Hook & The Problem
"Hey, [Name]. Thanks for taking the time. 

I'm going to get straight to it. If you’re running a solar sales team, you know the biggest bottleneck to scaling isn't getting leads—it's getting *reps ready to close those leads*.

The old way of training relies on handing new guys a PDF script, having them shadow a closer for a few days, and then throwing them to the wolves. It’s inconsistent. It burns through leads. And honestly, it burns out your managers who are trying to close their own deals while babysitting new hires. 

That’s exactly why I built SeptiVolt."

### [1:00 - 2:30] Introducing SeptiVolt & The Rep Experience
"SeptiVolt is an AI-powered Solar Sales Training Operating System. We don't just give your reps videos to watch; we force them to practice, and we grade them on it.

*(Share Screen: Show Rep Dashboard)*

Here’s what your rep sees day one. It's clean, it’s gamified. They go through the curriculum, but here’s where the magic happens: The AI Simulation.

Instead of practicing on your expensive leads, they practice on our AI."

### [2:30 - 4:30] AI Simulation & Coaching Report
*(Click into a simulation - e.g., 'The Spouse Objection')*

"Let's say they're learning to handle the 'I need to talk to my wife' objection. They hit start, and they actually have a conversation with an AI homeowner. The AI will interrupt them, it will throw curveballs, it acts like a real person. 

*(Show a brief interaction or explain it)*

When they finish, they don't just get a 'good job.' They get a detailed Coaching Debrief. 

*(Show Coaching Report)*

The AI breaks down exactly what they did right, what they missed in your specific framework, and gives them actionable feedback. They have to pass this to get certified. They do not touch a real homeowner until this screen says 'Passed'."

### [4:30 - 6:00] Manager Hub & Bilingual Advantage
"Now, if you're a manager, you don't have time to listen to 50 roleplay calls a day. 

*(Switch to Manager Hub)*

This is your command center. You get total visibility. You can see who is doing the work, who is slacking, and more importantly, where they are failing. If Jimmy fails the Net Metering pitch 3 times, you get a flag. You step in for 5 minutes and fix Jimmy, instead of waiting 3 weeks to realize he doesn't know what he's talking about.

Also, for those of you building diverse teams, SeptiVolt is fully bilingual. 

*(Toggle English/Spanish)*

Your Spanish-speaking reps get the exact same curriculum, the exact same AI roleplays, natively in Spanish. No one gets left behind."

Right now, SeptiVolt is in a powerful, early-access beta. The platform is highly functional, but we are actively iterating based on real feedback. 

Because of that, we are opening up a 'Founding Partner Pilot'."
