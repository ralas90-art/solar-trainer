# SeptiVolt: Demo Call Flow

## 1. Pre-Call Framing (2 mins)
*Goal: Take control and set the agenda.*
"Hey [Name], great to meet you. To respect your time, my goal today is simple: I want to show you how SeptiVolt is using AI to completely automate roleplaying and coaching for solar reps. If it's a fit, we can talk about our Pilot program. If not, no worries at all. Sound good?"

## 2. Discovery & Pain Amplification (5-7 mins)
*Goal: Find out how much manual training is costing them.*
- "How many reps are you actively trying to onboard right now?"
- "Walk me through your current training process. Day 1 to Day 30."
- "How much time are your managers spending doing manual roleplays?"
- "What happens when a rep gets on the doors or phones and they blow a basic objection like 'I need to talk to my spouse'? How do you currently fix that?"
- *Pain Amplification:* "So your managers are essentially sacrificing their own closing time to train guys who might quit in 3 weeks? That sounds exhausting."

## 3. Product Walkthrough (7-10 mins)
*Goal: Show, don't just tell. Focus on the 'Aha' moments.*
- **The Rep View:** Show the clean UI. "No more messy Google Drive folders."
- **The AI Roleplay (The Magic):** Run a quick simulation. Let the AI interrupt. "See how the AI acts like a real homeowner? Your reps practice here, not on your $100 leads."
- **The Coaching Debrief:** Show the instant feedback. "This is where the manager saves 10 hours a week. The AI grades the rep for you."
- **The Manager Hub:** Show the analytics. "You know exactly who is ready and who is faking it."
- **Bilingual Toggle (If applicable):** "Got Spanish speakers? One click, the whole system translates."

## 4. Manager ROI Framing (3 mins)
*Goal: Shift from 'cool tech' to 'business asset'.*
"Think about your best manager. If SeptiVolt can take the baseline training and roleplaying off their plate, how many more deals could they close themselves? This isn't just an app; it's a clone of your best trainer."

## 5. The Pilot Close (5 mins)
*Goal: Present the offer with conviction.*
"Look, [Name], the platform works. We are currently in our Beta rollout (v2.4.2), which means it's powerful, but we are still adding new features every week based on user feedback. 

Because of that, we aren't charging the standard $99/rep/month right now. We are doing a **Founding Partner Pilot**. 

We waive the setup fee, and it's a flat $499/mo for your whole team. In exchange, I just ask for your honest feedback to help us make the product perfect. We only have a few spots left for this onboarding cohort. 

Does it make sense to get your team set up for next week?"

## 6. Objection Handling
- **"It's too early/buggy."** -> "That's exactly why it's a flat rate instead of per-user. You get in on the ground floor, lock in the price, and you get to tell my dev team exactly what features to build for you."
- **"My reps won't use it."** -> "Make it mandatory. They don't get leads until their dashboard says 'Certified' by the AI. It polices itself."
- **"We have our own scripts."** -> "We can white-label and input your exact scripts into the AI evaluation criteria."
