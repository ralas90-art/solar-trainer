# SeptiVolt: Founding Partner Pilot Offer

## Positioning
**"The SeptiVolt Founding Partner Pilot"**
Positioned as an exclusive, early-access beta for forward-thinking solar organizations who want to shape the future of AI sales training.

## Who It's For
- Solar sales organizations (10+ reps)
- EPCs wanting to standardize dealer training
- Managers who are overwhelmed with manual roleplaying
- Teams actively recruiting and needing a scalable onboarding system

## What They Get
- **Full Access:** Unlocked access to the SeptiVolt OS (v2.4.2) for the whole team.
- **AI Roleplay Engine:** Unlimited practice simulations and coaching debriefs.
- **Manager Hub:** Full analytics and team visibility.
- **Bilingual Suite:** Access to both English and Spanish curriculums.
- **White-Glove Onboarding:** 2 hands-on implementation calls to set up their team.
- **Direct Roadmap Input:** Bi-weekly feedback loops directly with the founder to request specific features.

## What We Need From Them
- Commitment to run at least 5 reps through the curriculum in the first 14 days.
- One 30-minute feedback call per month.
- A video testimonial/case study upon successful reduction in ramp-time.

## Pricing Strategy (Early Access)
- **Standard (Future) Pricing:** $99/rep/month + $1,500 setup fee.
- **Pilot Pricing:** Waived setup fee. Flat rate of $499/month for the whole office (up to 20 reps) for the first 6 months. 
- *Note: Frame this as a massive discount in exchange for their feedback while the product stabilizes.*

## Scarcity / Urgency Angle
"Because this requires white-glove onboarding and direct founder access, we are strictly capping this Pilot at **10 companies**. We currently have 3 spots left for this month. Once v3.0 launches, prices move to the standard per-seat model."

## The Guarantee
"If your reps don't feel more confident and your managers don't save at least 10 hours a week on training in the first 30 days, we'll refund the pilot fee immediately."
