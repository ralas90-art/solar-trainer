# SeptiVolt: Influencer & Sales Trainer Partnership Pitch

This document details the outreach strategy and messaging templates for partnering with solar sales influencers, consultants, and independent trainers.

---

## 1. Why SeptiVolt Benefits Their Audience
*   **Actionable Practice:** Influencers teach frameworks; SeptiVolt lets their students *practice* them. It bridges the gap between learning a concept in a video/course and executing it under pressure.
*   **Branded Simulation Environment:** Trainers can load their proprietary sales methods (e.g., "The Cloverleaf Pitch" or "The avoiding-MPU framework") directly into our AI homeowner simulation criteria, creating a custom practice environment for their students.
*   **Scale Distribution:** Trainers can package their courses with a sandbox where students are certified ready via AI. It adds a premium software component to their consulting packages.

## 2. Affiliate / Pilot Partner Angle
*   **Distribution Partner:** Influencers earn a recurring 30% commission on every team subscription they refer.
*   **Beta/Pilot Access:** We offer influencers custom sandboxes where they can host their own training scripts, and let them invite their top student-run dealerships into the pilot.

## 6. Partnership Call Script (10-15 Minutes)
*   **Intro (2 mins):** Establish rapport. "Love the training program you've built. How are you currently tracking if your students are actually practicing the scripts you teach them?"
*   **The Gap (3 mins):** Highlight the execution gap. "Reps listen to your training, say 'that makes sense,' and then freeze on their first door knock. Managers don't have time to run 1-on-1 roleplays with everyone."
*   **The Pitch (5 mins):** Show the platform. Focus on the *Custom Frameworks* feature: "We can program the AI to evaluate reps specifically on your curriculum. You can sell your training + the practice software as a high-ticket bundle."
*   **The Close (3 mins):** Set up their partner account. "Let's set you up with a partner sandbox. I'll load your main door-opener script into the AI. Play with it this week, and if you like it, we can talk about introducing it to your client networks."
