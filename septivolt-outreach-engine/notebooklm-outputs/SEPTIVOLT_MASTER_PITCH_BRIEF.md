# SeptiVolt: Master Pitch Brief

## 1. What is SeptiVolt?
SeptiVolt is an AI-powered Solar Sales Training Operating System (OS). It is a complete readiness platform that replaces static PDF scripts and manual shadowing with interactive, conversational AI voice simulations, real-time coaching debriefs, and automated team performance tracking. It is built specifically to systemize training, accelerate representative ramp-up times, and protect marketing pipelines.

## 2. Who is it For?
*   **Solar Company Owners / Dealerships:** Seeking to scale operations without expanding administrative headcount or burning marketing capital on untrained reps.
*   **EPC Executives:** Seeking to standardize and maintain consistent training standards across their dealer networks.
*   **Sales Managers:** Overwhelmed by repetitive, manual roleplays and seeking to automate baseline training.
*   **Recruiting Managers:** Wanting to leverage a modern, tech-enabled onboarding environment to attract top-tier talent.
*   **Bilingual Team Leaders:** Managing diverse teams that require equivalent training in English and Spanish.

## 3. Why Solar Companies Need It
*   **The Inefficiency of Shadowing:** Traditional training relies heavily on ride-alongs and shadowing. This method is highly unscalable, inconsistent across offices, and drags top closers away from active deals.
*   **The Lead Burn Rate:** Handing live leads to undertrained representatives is an expensive mistake. Reps end up practicing on hot prospects, resulting in blown objections and lost revenue.
*   **High Early-Stage Turnover:** Roughly 80% of new solar representatives churn in their first 30 days. This turnover is largely driven by lack of confidence and poor preparation on doors and phones.

## 4. Main Pain Points Solved
*   **Undertrained Representatives:** Reps go into the field with a high risk of failure.
*   **Manager Burnout:** Sales managers spend hours conducting repetitive roleplays instead of closing deals or recruiting.
*   **Burned Marketing Capital:** Expensive leads are wasted as practice ground for new hires.
*   **Inconsistent Training:** Multi-office or decentralized teams lose pitch alignment.

## 5. The Core Product Loop
The primary representative experience on SeptiVolt follows a direct, gamified progression:
1.  **Learn the Framework:** Reps progress through structured curriculum lessons (covering mindset, time management, door interrupts, discovery, presentations, and objection turnarounds).
2.  **Practice Against AI:** Reps engage in realistic, conversational voice simulations with a customized AI homeowner (which interrupts, throws objections, and acts like a real buyer).
3.  **Instant Coaching Feedback:** Upon completing a simulation, the AI evaluates the conversation against the company's framework, providing a structured, granular coaching score and critique. The rep must achieve a passing grade before being cleared for the field.

## 6. The Manager Value
*   **Automated Coaching Debriefs:** Managers no longer have to manually listen to hours of roleplay audio. The AI handles the grading and feedback.
*   **The Attention Flag System:** If a representative fails a critical pitch segment (e.g., Net Metering or Tax Credits) multiple times, the Manager Hub flags it, allowing the manager to step in for a targeted 5-minute correction.
*   **Total Visibility:** A centralized dashboard provides team metrics, certification progress, and XP rankings, giving leaders total visibility into who is actually ready to sell.

## 7. The Bilingual Advantage
SeptiVolt is built natively as a bilingual platform:
*   **1:1 Translation:** Representatives can toggle the entire interface, lesson content, quizzes, and AI voice simulations between English and Spanish.
*   **Equal Standard:** Spanish-speaking reps get the exact same level of curriculum depth and interactive AI practice without requiring bilingual managers to conduct the training.
