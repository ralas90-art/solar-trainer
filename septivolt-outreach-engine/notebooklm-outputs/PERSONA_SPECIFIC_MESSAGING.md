# SeptiVolt: Persona-Specific Messaging Guide

This guide maps out the target pain points, emotional triggers, business outcomes, positioning angles, and outreach templates for SeptiVolt's primary buyer personas.

---

## 1. Solar Company Owner
*   **Main Pain:** High acquisition costs and lost revenue from burned leads (new reps blowing hot prospects).
*   **Emotional Trigger:** "My money is being flushed down the toilet because raw recruits are practicing on leads I paid $150 for."
*   **Business Outcome:** Increased office close rates, reduced lead acquisition waste, higher ROI on marketing spend.
*   **Best SeptiVolt Angle:** *Protecting the Pipeline.* Emphasize that reps must pass AI certification before receiving live leads.
*   **Outreach Message:** "Hey [Name], quick question: are your new sales hires practicing on your expensive, live leads? We built an AI training OS that roleplays with them so they only speak to real homeowners once they're certified ready. Open to seeing a 2-min demo?"
*   **CTA:** Reply for a quick Loom link.

## 2. EPC Executive
*   **Main Pain:** Inconsistent training quality across independent dealer networks, causing quality control and compliance issues.
*   **Emotional Trigger:** "My brand and installations are at risk because I cannot control how external dealer teams train their reps on my product."
*   **Business Outcome:** Standardized training quality, protected brand reputation, consistent sales volume across channels.
*   **Best SeptiVolt Angle:** *White-Labeled Standardization.* Position SeptiVolt as a white-labeled training platform the EPC hands to their dealer networks.
*   **Outreach Message:** "Hi [Name], as an EPC, your volume depends on your dealers' close rates. We built an AI training OS that you can white-label and hand to your dealers, guaranteeing their reps are certified on your guidelines before they submit a contract. Worth a 5-min look?"
*   **CTA:** Grab a quick Zoom time.

## 3. Sales Manager
*   **Main Pain:** Overwhelmed by repetitive, manual roleplays and riding shotgun with new hires, cutting into personal closing time.
*   **Emotional Trigger:** "I am acting as a babysitter and losing my personal commissions because I have to do basic roleplays and shadow rookies."
*   **Business Outcome:** Time saved (10+ hours/week), focus returned to high-ticket deals, clear team performance data.
*   **Best SeptiVolt Angle:** *Time Automation & Manager Hub.* Highlight automated grading and the performance flag system.
*   **Outreach Message:** "Hey [Name], quick question: how many hours a week are your managers spending running basic objection roleplays? We built SeptiVolt to automate that completely via AI voice simulations. Frees them up to close more deals. Worth a 5-min Loom?"
*   **CTA:** Send the video link.

## 4. Recruiting Manager
*   **Main Pain:** High new-hire churn (80% in the first 30 days) and difficulty attracting top-tier talent without a clear structure.
*   **Emotional Trigger:** "I recruit 20 people and 15 quit in week two because they got slammed on the doors and lost confidence."
*   **Business Outcome:** Reduced new-hire turnover, lower recruitment costs, strong brand positioning to recruit talent.
*   **Best SeptiVolt Angle:** *Modern Training Environment.* Highlight the gamified dashboard and structured onboarding tracks as a recruiting asset.
*   **Outreach Message:** "Hi [Name], seeing the hiring push at [Company]. We built a gamified AI training platform that gets recruits certified ready in 14 days, reducing new hire churn by building their confidence in simulated environments first. Open to checking it out?"
*   **CTA:** Book a demo.

## 5. Solar Influencer / Sales Trainer
*   **Main Pain:** Difficulty scaling training services beyond 1-on-1 calls; students fail to execute concepts taught in courses.
*   **Emotional Trigger:** "My clients aren't getting results because their reps listen to my videos but freeze when they knocked on the door."
*   **Business Outcome:** New recurring SaaS revenue (30% affiliate share), scalable practice tool added to training packages.
*   **Best SeptiVolt Angle:** *Interactive Practice Sandbox.* Highlight loading *their* custom scripts into the AI evaluation criteria.
*   **Outreach Message:** "Hey [Name], love your content on door openers. We built an AI training simulator that lets reps voice-practice objections. We want to build you a custom sandbox to load your scripts so your students can practice *your* method. Open to a quick chat?"
*   **CTA:** Jump on a 10-minute partnership call.

## 6. Bilingual Team Leader
*   **Main Pain:** Inability to provide high-quality, equivalent sales training and AI roleplays for Spanish-speaking reps.
*   **Emotional Trigger:** "My Spanish-speaking reps are underserved because all our materials and my managers are English-centric."
*   **Business Outcome:** Equal sales execution across English and Spanish teams, unlocked market share in Hispanic communities.
*   **Best SeptiVolt Angle:** *Full Bilingual Toggle.* Focus on the 1:1 equivalent English & Spanish curriculum and native AI voice simulations.
*   **Outreach Message:** "Hey [Name], the Hispanic solar market is booming, but training Spanish reps can be a bottleneck. We built a fully bilingual AI training platform—reps can toggle curriculum and voice roleplay to Spanish in one click. Open to a quick demo?"
*   **CTA:** Reply for a demo link.

## 7. New Rep Onboarding Director
*   **Main Pain:** Inconsistent onboarding timelines; some reps take 6+ weeks to close their first deal, draining cash flow.
*   **Emotional Trigger:** "I am struggling to manage onboarding logistics, and I don't know who is actually ready to sell until they blow a live deal."
*   **Business Outcome:** Accelerated time-to-first-deal (under 14 days), standardized onboarding path, total readiness metrics.
*   **Best SeptiVolt Angle:** *Certified Field Readiness.* Focus on the structured modules, quizzes, and automated field clearance badges.
*   **Outreach Message:** "Hi [Name], if it takes your new hires 6 weeks to close their first deal, your onboarding is leaking revenue. We built an AI OS that cuts ramp time in half by certifying reps via voice simulation before they knock. Open to a quick Loom?"
*   **CTA:** Send the Loom.
