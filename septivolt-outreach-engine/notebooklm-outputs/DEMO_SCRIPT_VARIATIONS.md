# SeptiVolt: Demo Script Variations

These three script variations are designed to sound natural, authoritative, and founder-led, speaking directly to solar sales leadership.

---

I’m reaching out because if you run a solar team at [Company Name], you know the biggest bottleneck to scaling isn't getting leads—it's getting your reps ready to close those leads.

Let's be honest. The old way of training is broken. Handing new hires a PDF script and having them shadow a closer for a few days does not work. Reps end up practicing on your expensive leads, blowing basic objections, and eventually quitting in their first 30 days because they lose confidence.

We built SeptiVolt to solve this.

*(Share Screen: Show Rep Dashboard and click into AI Roleplay)*

This is our AI Solar Sales Training OS. Instead of reps reading scripts, they practice directly against our AI homeowner. The AI acts like a real homeowner: it interrupts, sounds skeptical, and throws standard door-to-door or phone objections in real-time. 

*(Run a quick 10-second voice demo, showing the AI interrupting)*

When the rep finishes, they don't just get a pat on the back. The AI generates this instant Coaching Debrief. It scores them from 0 to 100 on script compliance, tone, and objection handling. They have to pass the AI before they ever speak to a real prospect.

*(Switch to Manager Hub)*

And for you as a leader, this Manager Hub gives you total X-ray vision. You see exactly who is ready and who is struggling, saving your managers 10+ hours a week on manual roleplays. It's even fully bilingual in English and Spanish.

We're opening up 3 more spots in our Founding Partner Pilot, where we waive setup fees and offer flat office pricing.

If you want to cut your rep ramp-up time in half, grab 10 minutes on my calendar below and I’ll set you up with a free trial.

Keep crushing it. Talk soon."

## Variation 2: The 7-Minute Sales Demo (Standard Zoom Demo)
**Audience:** Scheduled demo bookings.  
**Goal:** Build rapport, amplify pain, demonstrate core value, close on the pilot.

### [0:00 - 1:30] Agenda & Discovery
"Hey [Name], great to meet you. Today, my goal is simple: I want to show you how SeptiVolt uses conversational AI to automate sales roleplays, grading, and certifications. If it's a fit, we can talk about the Pilot. If not, no worries at all.

Before I share my screen, how are you currently onboarding new hires? And how much time are your managers spending doing manual roleplays?"

### [1:30 - 4:00] The Rep Loop & AI Roleplay
"Let's look at the rep experience. 

*(Share Screen: Show Rep Dashboard)*

We replace Google Drive folders with structured lessons. But the core is the AI Simulator. Reps practice against our AI homeowner. They speak naturally, and the AI responds.

*(Click into a D2D scenario: 'The Spouse Objection'. Run a short interaction)*

Notice how the AI sounds skeptical? We train the model on real homeowner conversations. It doesn't just read lines—it reacts to the rep's actual words.

*(Show the Coaching Report)*

Once completed, the AI grades the conversation. It checks if they used the empathy pivot, if they qualified the utility bill, and if they set the appointment. Reps have to repeat this until they pass. This prevents them from practicing on your expensive marketing leads."

### [4:00 - 5:30] The Manager Hub & Bilingual Suite
"Now, let's look at leadership visibility.

*(Switch to Manager Hub)*

Managers don't have time to listen to roleplays. This dashboard compiles the data. If a rep fails the Net Metering quiz 3 times, it flags it. You step in for a targeted 5-minute correction. 

Also, if you manage a bilingual team, the entire platform—curriculum and voice roleplay—translates to Spanish with one toggle."

### [5:30 - 7:00] The Pilot Pitch & Close
"SeptiVolt is currently in our early-access beta (v2.4.2). The core system is highly functional, and we are constantly adding custom features for our partners.

Because of this, we are running a Founding Partner Pilot. We waive the $1,500 setup fee and offer flat-rate pricing of $499/mo for your whole team, instead of our standard seat pricing.

In exchange, we want your feedback to make the product perfect. We have 3 spots left for this cohort. Does it make sense to get your team onboarded for next week?"

## Variation 3: The 15-Minute Founder Walkthrough
**Audience:** EPC Owners, large dealer groups, or strategic partners.  
**Goal:** Deep dive into product architecture, security, customization, and long-term vision.

### [0:00 - 3:00] Introduction & Vision
"Thanks for jumping on. As the founder, my vision for SeptiVolt is to build the default Operating System for solar training. 

We aren't just building a roleplay bot; we are building an integrated compliance and readiness engine. In solar, dealer networks fluctuate, close rates dictate volume, and training quality is a constant risk. SeptiVolt standardizes that quality at scale."

### [3:00 - 8:00] Deep-Dive Technical Walkthrough
*(Share Screen: Walk through full platform)*
*   **The Diagnostic Onboarding:** "When a rep first joins, the system runs an interview to assess their experience level and routes them to the appropriate curriculum tier."
*   **AI Simulation Variations:** "We have 10+ active scenarios representing different buyer types (annoyed homeowner, utility auditor, CFO type) and state-specific contexts (California NEM 3.0 vs. New York VDER)."
*   **The Custom Criteria Engine:** "In our Admin Hub, we can ingest your company's proprietary sales playbooks. The AI evaluates reps specifically on *your* steps, not generic guidelines."

### [8:00 - 11:00] Enterprise Readiness & Infrastructure
"We know enterprise teams require security and stability. In our latest build (v2.4.2), we’ve focused heavily on:
*   **Data Security:** Implementing strict Row-Level Security (RLS) across all tenants to guarantee your sales data is isolated.
*   **Query Performance:** Overhauling DB indexes and pre-warming endpoints so pages load instantly during active field use.
*   **Integrations:** Webhook support to push certification data directly into GHL pipelines or custom CRMs."

### [11:00 - 15:00] Commercial Model & Strategic Partnership
"Our standard pricing is $99/rep/seat. However, we are onboarding our final Founding Partner Pilot cohort.

For $499/month, we will white-label a subdomain for your organization, customize the AI with your scripts, and give you direct roadmap access. 

Let's discuss how we can customize this for your organization's workflow."
