# SeptiVolt: Demo Call Battlecard (Live Zoom Demos)

## 1. Opening Hook (First 2 Minutes)
"Hey [Name], great to meet you. To respect your time, let's keep this direct. 

My goal today is simple: I want to show you how SeptiVolt is using conversational AI to automate roleplaying, coaching, and certification for solar representatives. If you see the value and it looks like a fit, we can talk about getting your team into our early-access Pilot. If not, no worries at all. 

Does that sound like a fair use of your time?"

## 2. Discovery Questions (5-7 Minutes)
Use these questions to uncover key operational bottlenecks:
*   *Team Growth:* "How many new reps are you actively trying to onboard and get ready for the field this quarter?"
*   *Current Pipeline:* "Walk me through your current training process. Day 1 to Day 30—what do they do?"
*   *Management Headcount:* "How much time are your sales managers currently spending doing manual, repetitive roleplays and door-shadowing with new hires?"
*   *Field Readiness:* "What happens right now when a new rep gets on the doors or on a Zoom and blows a basic objection like 'I need to talk to my spouse'? How does that get fixed, and what does it cost in burned leads?"

## 3. Pain Points to Listen For (Diagnostic Flags)
*   **"We have high rep turnover."** (Trigger: Reps quit because they feel unprepared and lose confidence early on).
*   **"My managers are too busy."** (Trigger: Managers are active closers and their personal sales volume drops when they spend time training recruits).
*   **"Reps are burning through our leads."** (Trigger: Sales reps are practicing their pitch on hot, expensive leads).
*   **"Training is inconsistent."** (Trigger: Script delivery varies wildly between offices or managers).

## 4. The Demo Path & Key Product Moments (7-10 Minutes)
Show, don't tell. Walk the prospect through these core UI sections:
*   **The Representative Dashboard:** Show the clean, MasterClass-style gamified lesson modules. Emphasize that all learning paths are structured, leaving no room for "Google Drive chaos."
*   **The AI Simulation Panel:** Run a 1-minute live voice roleplay (e.g., *The Spouse Objection* or *Busy Saturday Morning*). 
    *   *Highlight:* "Notice how the AI homeowner interrupts the rep, sounds annoyed, and throws real objection curveballs. The rep practices here, not on your $100 leads."
*   **The AI Coaching Debrief:** Show the immediate scorecard, highlight the specific categories (e.g., Tone, Adherence to Script, Objections Met).
    *   *Highlight:* "The AI grades the call instantly. They cannot advance or go to the doors until the system certifies them as 'Passed'."
*   **The Manager Hub:** Show the team ranking, progress charts, and active performance alerts.
    *   *Highlight:* "Total X-ray vision. If Jimmy fails the Net Metering pitch 3 times in a row, it flags him. You spend 5 minutes correcting Jimmy instead of waiting 3 weeks for his numbers to drop."
*   **The Bilingual Toggle:** Toggle the interface from English to Spanish.
    *   *Highlight:* "Got Spanish-speaking reps? One click translates the entire curriculum and voice roleplay native to the system."

## 5. Manager ROI Framing (3 Minutes)
"Think about your top-producing manager. If SeptiVolt handles the baseline training, roleplays, and grading for your recruits, how many more hours can they dedicate to high-level recruiting and closing deals themselves? This doesn't cost you money—it clones your best trainer and frees up your closers."

## 6. The Pilot Close (5 Minutes)
"Look, [Name], the platform is fully functional, but we are actively iterating based on real user feedback as we stabilize our v2.4.2 enterprise build. 

Because of that, we are waiving our standard $1,500 setup fee and offering a flat rate of **$499/month** for your entire office (up to 20 reps) instead of the standard $99/rep/seat pricing.

In exchange, we just ask for your honest feedback and a case study once we cut your team's ramp-up time in half. We're strictly capping this pilot cohort at 10 companies to keep onboarding white-glove, and we have 3 spots left.

Should we get your team set up on the platform for next week?"
