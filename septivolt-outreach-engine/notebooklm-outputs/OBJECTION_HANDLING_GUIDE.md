# SeptiVolt: Objection Handling Guide

This guide details the strategic, honest responses to use when addressing prospects' objections. We position SeptiVolt as functional, pilot-ready, and rapidly developing, without overclaiming enterprise maturity.

---

### 1. "We already train our reps."
*   **Response:** "That’s awesome. Honestly, if you didn't have a training process, you wouldn't be in business. We aren't here to replace your training materials or your culture. What we do is automate the *delivery* and the *practice* of that training. Instead of your reps memorizing your scripts by reading a PDF or practicing on live leads, they practice against our AI. Think of it as a flight simulator that sits on top of your existing curriculum to guarantee reps can execute it before they go live."

### 2. "We use ride-alongs."
*   **Response:** "Ride-alongs are crucial for building team culture, but they don't scale. If a manager is spending 20 hours a week in a passenger seat shadowing a rookie, that's 20 hours they aren't spending closing deals or recruiting. Plus, ride-alongs mean your reps are learning in front of real customers, which leads to burned prospects. We use AI roleplays to get reps to 80% readiness *before* they ever step into a car, saving your managers' time for high-leverage field coaching."

### 3. "Our managers handle training."
*   **Response:** "Your managers are likely your best closers, which makes training a double-edged sword. Every hour they spend running basic roleplays is an hour of lost personal sales volume. SeptiVolt automates the repetitive baseline roleplays and grading. The AI handles the drill work, and the Manager Hub alerts your managers exactly when and where a rep is struggling (e.g., failing the Net Metering pitch). This allows managers to step in for a targeted 5-minute correction rather than a 2-hour lecture."

### 4. "We don’t need AI."
*   **Response:** "I get it—there is a lot of generic AI hype out there. We didn't build SeptiVolt to be a flashy chatbot. We built it to solve a math problem: the cost of burned leads and the long ramp-up times of new reps. If your close rate is 15% and you can get it to 18% because your reps handled the 'talking to the spouse' objection correctly on their first door, that represents a massive addition to your bottom line. The AI is just the technology that makes that practice scale."

### 5. "Our reps won’t use another platform."
*   **Response:** "Reps hate systems that slow them down, but they love systems that help them make money. SeptiVolt is mobile-friendly and highly gamified, designed with a MasterClass-style interface. More importantly, most of our pilot partners make passing the AI simulation a requirement before reps get leads. Once they realize that 30 minutes in the simulator saves them from getting slammed on the doors, adoption takes care of itself."

### 6. "How do I know this improves sales?"
*   **Response:** "The metric we track is 'Time to First Deal.' In solar, if a rep takes 6 weeks to close their first deal, they are highly likely to quit. By forcing them to drill objection handling and pitches against the AI before going out, they hit the doors with confidence. Our goal is to cut the standard 6-week ramp down to 14 days. We track performance data, and we will look at your close rates and ramp times together during the monthly pilot feedback calls."

### 7. "Is this ready for enterprise?"
*   **Response:** "We want to be completely transparent: our active platform build (v2.4.2) is a robust, functional release, but we are actively developing and optimizing features. We are currently stabilizing performance (we recently overhauled login latency and added non-blocking skeletons to the dashboard) and security (such as strict Row-Level Security isolation). It is fully ready to train teams of 10 to 50 reps today, and our pilot partners get direct access to our roadmap to request custom integrations as we scale."

### 8. "Do you integrate with our CRM?"
*   **Response:** "We support custom webhook integrations and are actively building out native CRM sync workflows (such as GoHighLevel) to track representative certification states. During our Founding Partner Pilot, we provide white-glove setup and will work directly with your team to configure webhook integrations so that reps' training progress is pushed straight to your management pipeline."

### 9. "Do you support Spanish reps?"
*   **Response:** "Yes, natively. SeptiVolt is fully bilingual. With a single toggle, the rep gets the exact same curriculum, quizzes, and AI voice roleplay simulations in Spanish. This allows you to scale Spanish-speaking sales teams consistently, even if your managers don't speak Spanish."

### 10. "What happens after the pilot?"
*   **Response:** "As a Founding Partner, you lock in your pilot rate ($499/mo for the whole office, up to 20 reps) for the first 6 months. Once the beta phase is complete and we transition to standard seat-based pricing ($99/rep/mo + setup fee), you will have the option to transition to our Growth or Enterprise tiers with grandfathered discounts. We want to reward the teams that helped us build and test the platform early on."
