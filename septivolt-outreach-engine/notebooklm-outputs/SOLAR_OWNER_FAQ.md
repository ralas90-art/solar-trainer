This landing-page-ready FAQ answers common questions from solar company owners, EPC executives, sales managers, and recruiting directors.

### General & Onboarding
#### How long does it take to onboard my team onto SeptiVolt?
We provide white-glove onboarding for all pilot partners. Getting your team set up takes less than 48 hours. We configure your company settings, invite your representatives, and walk your managers through the analytics dashboard during two direct implementation calls.

#### Can we customize the training with our own scripts and frameworks?
Yes. Our platform supports company-specific customization. We work with you to white-label the environment and input your team's specific scripts (e.g., door openers, discovery patterns, closing logic) directly into the AI evaluation criteria.

### Simulation & Coaching
#### How do the AI simulations work?
Reps practice by engaging in live voice conversations with an AI homeowner. The AI is trained specifically on solar sales contexts: it interrupts, throws objections (e.g., "talking to a spouse", "NEM 3.0", "moving soon"), and mimics realistic door-to-door or phone call scenarios. Reps speak naturally, and the AI responds in real-time.

#### What is included in the Coaching Reports?
Upon completing a simulation, the representative receives a detailed, automated Coaching Debrief. The AI grades their performance on a scale of 0-100 and evaluates critical dimensions such as objection resolution, script adherence, tone, and pacing. It provides specific, actionable feedback on what they did right and exactly what they need to fix.

### Certifications & Management
#### How do Certifications work?
SeptiVolt features a structured, modular certification track. Reps progress through curriculum lessons and must pass corresponding AI simulations to unlock subsequent modules. Before being cleared for the doors or phones, reps must complete their final assessment and earn their certified badge.

#### What does the Manager Hub track?
The Manager Hub acts as your command center. It tracks daily team activity, lesson progress, simulation scores, and leaderboard rankings. It also features an automated flag system: if a rep repeatedly fails a specific pitch topic (e.g., Net Metering), it highlights the gap, allowing managers to execute targeted coaching.

### Bilingual & White-Label Support
#### Do you support Spanish-speaking reps?
Yes, natively. SeptiVolt is 100% bilingual. With a single click, representatives can toggle the entire platform—including training modules, quizzes, and the conversational AI voice roleplay simulations—between English and Spanish. This ensures consistent training standards across diverse offices.

#### Can we white-label the platform?
Yes. For EPCs, dealer networks, and large organizations, we offer custom white-labeled environments featuring your logo, brand colors, and custom subdomains, establishing a premium, enterprise-ready look for your recruiting and onboarding pipelines.

#### What is the Founding Partner Pilot?
The pilot is an early-access program for forward-thinking solar teams. It is capped at 10 companies, allowing us to work hands-on with owners to refine the system as we stabilize our v2.4.2 enterprise build. 

#### How much does SeptiVolt cost during the Pilot?
During the pilot, we waive the standard $1,500 setup fee. We offer flat-rate office pricing of **$499/month** for the entire team (up to 20 seats) for the first 6 months. Once the beta phase completes, pricing moves to our standard seat-based model ($99/rep/month).

Yes. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If your representatives do not show measurable improvement in confidence or your managers do not save at least 10 hours a week on manual training in the first 30 days, we will refund your pilot fee immediately.
