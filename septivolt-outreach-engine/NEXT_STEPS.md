# SeptiVolt: What to build first this week to start outreach immediately.

To get the outreach engine running within the next 48 hours, focus strictly on these priority execution items:

### Priority 1: Setup the Conversion Mechanism
1. **Build the Landing Page:** Use `LANDING_PAGE_COPY.md` to quickly spin up a one-pager on your domain (e.g., in GHL or Vercel/Next.js). Ensure the calendar embed for booking demos is live.
2. **Configure GHL Calendar:** Create the "SeptiVolt Demo" calendar in GoHighLevel to capture bookings.

### Priority 2: Prepare the Demo Assets
1. **Record the Loom:** Use `LOOM_SCRIPT.md` to record a universal 2-minute teaser video. Host it on Loom or YouTube (unlisted) so you have a link ready for DMs and Emails.
2. **Polish the Sales Deck:** Open Canva/Figma/PowerPoint and translate `SALES_DECK_OUTLINE.md` into actual visual slides. You need this ready for when they actually show up to the Zoom.

### Priority 3: Activate the GHL Automation
1. **Build the GHL Workflows:** Implement Workflow 1 (Cold Outreach) and Workflow 2 (Demo Booked) from `GHL_WORKFLOW_PLAN.md`. Keep it simple—just ensure they get reminder emails when they book.

### Priority 4: Launch Outreach (Start Hitting Send)
1. **Start DMs:** Take the scripts from `OUTREACH_MESSAGES.md` and start manually DMing 20 solar founders/managers a day on LinkedIn and Instagram. 
2. **Post Content:** Drop your first 'Build in Public' or 'Practice Hook' post from `CONTENT_ENGINE.md` to start generating organic curiosity.

**Rule of Thumb for this week:** Don't get stuck optimizing the platform code (v2.4.2). The product is good enough to demo. Spend 80% of your time this week driving traffic to the calendar using the Loom video and the Pilot Offer.
