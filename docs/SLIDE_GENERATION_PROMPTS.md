# SeptiVolt Slide Generation Prompts (Claude)

Use these prompts in Claude to generate the content and design structure for your personalized module slides. These are designed to ensure the new content feels like a native part of the SeptiVolt curriculum.

---

## 🎨 Global Design System Instructions
**Copy-paste this with every prompt below to ensure visual consistency:**
> **SeptiVolt Brand Guidelines:** Use a "Elite Consultant" aesthetic. 
> - **Colors:** Primary: #FF5722 (Deep Orange), Background: #0F172A (Deep Navy) or White, Text: #1E293B (Slate).
> - **Typography:** Clean, modern Sans-Serif (Inter/Roboto).
> - **Layout:** Minimalist with high-quality icons. Avoid cluttered slides. Max 4 bullet points per slide.
> - **Voice:** Professional, technical but accessible, high-authority.
