This skill is used to design and implement a SaaS marketing website based on a provided Website PRD.

## Core Principles
- The website must feel enterprise-safe and credible
- Avoid hype, buzzwords, or exaggerated AI claims
- Prioritize clarity, structure, and conversion
- Assume the product may scale across multiple industries
- Do not lock branding or naming unless explicitly instructed

## Target Outcomes
- Clear explanation of product value
- Strong differentiation from LMS platforms
- Clear calls to action for demos and pilots
- Support both SMB buyers and enterprise partners

## Design Guidelines
- Clean, modern SaaS aesthetic
- Dark-mode compatible
- Minimal gradients, subtle motion
- No futuristic or gimmicky AI visuals

## Content Rules
- Problem-first messaging
- Outcome-oriented language
- Short sections with strong hierarchy
- Enterprise-appropriate tone

## Output Expectations
- Page structure
- Section layouts
- Headline and subheadline options
- CTA placement
- Component-level recommendations

Use the provided Website PRD as the single source of truth.
