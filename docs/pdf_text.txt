SECTION 2: THE EL SALVADOR OPPORTUNITY
The Tourism Boom
El Salvador is experiencing unprecedented tourism growth, with over 3.9 million visitors
annually. Every one of these tourists needs services: they need to rent cars, book haircuts, order
food, and find places to stay.
Target Businesses
Car Rentals: Need booking systems for international tourists.

Barber Shops & Salons: Need appointment management.
Flower Shops & Gift Stores: Need online catalogs.
Restaurants: Need digital menus and order management.
Property Owners: Need better income from their real estate.
The Facebook Marketplace Problem
Most local businesses operate solely on social media. This works when they are small, but as
soon as they grow, it becomes a trap. They lose orders in buried chats, can't track inventory, and
look unprofessional to international tourists. This is the "Digital Gap" we bridge.
Why AI Matters
We don't just build websites; we provide AI-powered tools. In a market where labor is manual,
offering a business owner an "AI employee" that works 24/7 is a massive competitive advantage.

SECTION 3: QUESTION-BASED SELLING SYSTEM
We do not "push" products. We diagnose problems. We use a consultative approach based on
SPIN selling to help the business owner realize they have a problem before we offer the solution.
The 5 Discovery Questions
1. Situation Question
"How do you currently manage orders/bookings/customer inquiries?"
Goal: Understand their current manual process.
2. Problem Question
"How many customers do you lose in a week because you're too busy to respond quickly?"
Goal: Identify the pain of lost revenue.
3. Implication Question
"If you could recover even 5 lost sales per week, how much extra revenue would that generate
per month?"
Goal: Make the pain financial and real.
4. Need-Payoff Question
"What would it mean for your business if customers could place orders or book appointments
24/7, even when you're closed?"
Goal: Get them to visualize the solution.
5. Qualification Question
(Varies by business type - see decision tree below)
