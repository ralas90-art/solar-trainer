Appendix: Slides with No Image Opportunity
The following slides are dense data/table layouts where an image would compete with the core content. These are intentionally excluded from the image placement plan:

Photorealistic editorial photograph. Aspect ratio 16:9 unless otherwise noted. Color palette aligned with the Solar Sales Training learning platform: warm orange (#F4821F) and gold (#F5C842) accents are acceptable but image should NOT feel artificially tinted. Deep navy (#0D1F3C) backgrounds acceptable for scenario-type shots. Clean, modern, professional — no stock-photo cheese. No text, logos, or watermarks inside the image. Lighting: bright natural daylight preferred; avoid harsh flash. Diversity: represent a range of ethnicities, ages, genders. Always output 1792 × 1024 px (16:9) unless the slot is explicitly labeled "square" or "portrait."
